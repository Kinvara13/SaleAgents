Bidding Document - Smart Park Security System

Project: Smart Park Security Platform Construction
Client: Test Client Co., Ltd.

Chapter 1: Project Overview
This project aims to build a comprehensive security management platform for smart parks including video surveillance, access control, alarm systems and intelligent analytics.

Chapter 2: Technical Requirements
2.1 System shall support 1000+ concurrent video streams
2.2 Response time shall be less than 200ms
2.3 System availability shall be 99.9%
2.4 Data retention period: 90 days minimum

Chapter 3: Qualification Requirements
3.1 ISO 9001 certification required
3.2 ISO 27001 certification required
3.3 Minimum 3 years experience in similar projects
3.4 Registered capital shall be no less than 10 million RMB

Chapter 4: Evaluation Criteria
4.1 Technical solution: 40 points
4.2 Company qualification: 20 points
4.3 Price: 30 points
4.4 After-sales service: 10 points
